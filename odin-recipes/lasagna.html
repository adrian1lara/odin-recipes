Lasagna

Description

This is a lasagna recipe that is very easy to make and delicious

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Spices
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Cheeses
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat souce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the sauce
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
